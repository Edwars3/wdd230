Overview

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to expand our market providing our customers the opportunity to obtain packages for their vacations using adventure activities that we can offer to them. Another aspect is to provide support and information about our activities 24 hours. One important thing about this website is allow to the customer to share their perception about their experiences with the adventures that they will have getting our packages. The comments and reviews on our website will be used as a tool to convince other customers in the market to contact us to give them an unforgettable experience on their vacations.

Scenarios

The major part of the market that we pretend to reach are those how are looking to have a different vacation full of adventures. They are people between 11 and 50 years, much of the families or group of families that would like to have different vacations with activities that they enjoy with all the members of the family. They will have a good economic situation to plan vacations of at least a week with your family. Also, they are looking for the experience that we offer because that gives to them the calm that they will enjoy and will be safe because are being attended by trained people. Most visitors will use their phones to visit our website.

Wireframes

Wireframes are like blueprints for making webpages. They should show the major sections of content that will be on the page and the relative locations of each element. In the wireframe below you can see there will be 6 sections to our page:

  1. At the top we have a section with the logo (the box with the mountain means an image) and the navigation bar.
  2. Then there is a banner image that stretches all the way across the screen.
  3. Next we have some text and an image
  4. ...followed by another row made up of an image and some text.
  5. Then one more section of text with no image.
  6. Lastly, a footer containing a copyright/name line and 3 social media icons.
